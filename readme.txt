The following project has one source file (InventorySystem.cpp) and 4 .txt files apart from this
file. The rest of the .txt files are used to store data. The schema of the files is as follows:

admin.txt:
    <id>
    <name>
    <password>
    <id>
    <name>
    <password>
        .
        .
        .

book.txt:
    <title>
    <author>
    <publisher>
    <stock count>
    <availability>
    <price>
    <title>
    <author>
    <publisher>
    <stock count>
    <availability>
    <price>
        .
        .
        .

customer.txt:
    <name>
    <email>
    <contact>
    <total purchase>
    <name>
    <email>
    <contact>
    <total purchase>
        .
        .
        .

employee.txt:
    <id>
    <password>
    <name>
    <job title>
    <salary>
    <id>
    <password>
    <name>
    <job title>
    <salary>
        .
        .
        .
